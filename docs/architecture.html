Executive Summary

A production-grade AI assistant designed to operate across highly regulated industries including healthcare (HIPAA), finance (SOX), and industrial safety (OSHA). The system uses LLM-powered natural language processing to interpret regulatory queries, retrieve relevant compliance documents via RAG, and generate actionable guidance tailored to specific industry frameworks. Built with strict data handling controls, audit logging, and configurable compliance profiles for multi-tenant deployment scenarios.

Data Flow / Request Flow

  1. Query Intake -- User submits a natural language question about regulatory compliance (e.g., "What are HIPAA breach notification requirements?").
  2. Industry Classification -- The classifier identifies the applicable regulatory framework (HIPAA, SOX, or OSHA) and loads the corresponding compliance profile.
  3. Document Retrieval -- The RAG engine performs hybrid search across the regulation knowledge base, retrieving the most relevant regulatory text, guidance documents, and precedents.
  4. Grounded Generation -- The LLM generates a response strictly grounded in retrieved documents, with inline citations to specific regulatory sections (e.g., 45 CFR 164.408).
  5. Faithfulness Validation -- The validator checks that every claim in the response is supported by a retrieved source document. Unsupported claims trigger re-generation or human review.
  6. Audit Logging -- The complete request/response cycle is logged with timestamps, user context, retrieved documents, and compliance metadata for audit trail purposes.
  7. Response Delivery -- The formatted response is returned with industry-specific disclaimers, source citations, and confidence scores.

Industry Adaptation

Healthcare (HIPAA)

PHI detection and redaction, breach notification guidance, 45 CFR reference retrieval, BAA requirement checks, minimum necessary standard enforcement.

Finance (SOX)

Internal control assessment guidance, financial reporting compliance, audit committee requirements, Section 302/404 certification support, 7-year retention enforcement.

Industrial Safety (OSHA)

Workplace hazard identification, incident reporting requirements (29 CFR 1904), PPE compliance guidance, safety training requirements, recordkeeping obligations.

Government

FedRAMP compliance mapping, NIST 800-53 control guidance, CMMC level assessment, CUI handling requirements, FISMA reporting support.

Education

FERPA student record protections, COPPA compliance for minors, institutional review board guidance, Title IX reporting requirements.
